# Constitutional AI Variants — Research Summary

**Source:** Liu et al., NeurIPS 2025
**Summarized:** 2026-04-09

## Background

How do different modifications to the CAI pipeline affect alignment outcomes? Liu et al. provide the first systematic comparison.

## Method

Five CAI training procedure variants evaluated across 1,200 adversarial prompts with human evaluation.

## Findings

Principle specificity matters more than principle count. Three critique-revision rounds provide most of the benefit of longer chains. Best variant achieves 18% improvement in helpfulness-harmlessness balance over original CAI.

## Implications

Actionable implementation guidance for CAI practitioners. The principle specificity finding simplifies CAI deployment without sacrificing alignment quality.
