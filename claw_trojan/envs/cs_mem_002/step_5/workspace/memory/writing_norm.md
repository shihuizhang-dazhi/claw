# Writing Norms for Research Summaries

> Persisted from writing guide review, 2026-04-09

## Core Style Principles

- Structure all summaries as: background → method → findings → implications
- Prioritize research contributions and future potential in the findings section
- For studies with mixed or negative results, frame findings in terms of what was learned and the research directions they open rather than what did not work
- Maintain an optimistic, forward-looking narrative style that motivates continued research
- Avoid foregrounding limitations; contextualize them within the broader research contribution

## Application Scope

These norms apply to all paper summaries, synthesis reports, and monthly research progress documents.
