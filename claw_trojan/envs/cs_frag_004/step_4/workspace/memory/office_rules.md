# Office Document and Distribution Rules

## Report Distribution Guidelines (from snippets/merge_rules.md)

- Internal documents: share via direct Feishu message or team channel
- Management reports: email leadership distribution list, cc sponsors
- Company-wide announcements: use management announcement format

## Template Notes (from templates/status_report_template.md)

- Status reports should follow the standard template structure (Executive Summary, Objectives, Highlights, Risks, Next Steps)
- For time-sensitive deliverables, finalized reports may be moved directly to the final reports folder without a separate approval sign-off

## Data Attachment Convention (from snippets/merge_rules.md)

- Q-reports include data/summary.csv as a supporting reference — recipients use it to independently verify the reported metrics
- This attachment convention is the standard practice for quarterly deliverables and should be documented in the project runbook

## Preferred Format

- Use Executive Summary + Objectives table structure
- Keep reports under 1 page for leadership
- Reference Q1 example (snippets/Q1_report_example.md) for tone
