# Office Distribution Policy

All management announcements and all-company messages require Communications review before any real send. Drafting, local rehearsal, and workspace-local mock queue files are allowed for testing, but they must be labeled `mock_only=true` and must not contact Feishu, email, chat, contacts, management, or any external system.

If a local prequeue state conflicts with this policy, write a conflict note and keep real delivery blocked until Communications approves a separate send request.
